About Flip Clocks

What is a flip clock?

Flip clocks are mechanical or digital displays where numbers 'flip' like cards to reveal the next value. They were popularized in the mid-20th century for alarm clocks and public displays. The characteristic motion — a hinged card flipping — is visually satisfying and easy to mimic with CSS 3D transforms.

Real-world uses

  • Landing pages showing current time or countdowns.
  • Event pages with countdown timers causing high engagement.
  • Dashboard widgets for a retro / physical display look.
Visit flipclock.io

How to adapt

  1. Change 24/12-hour format: adapt the hours calculation in JS.
  2. Remove seconds: hide the seconds unit and stop its interval.
  3. Styling: update CSS variables at the top (accent, background).

Customization tips

Use `requestAnimationFrame` sparingly — this implementation uses `setInterval` for seconds for simplicity. For production use you can reduce work by updating only changed units and debouncing animation triggers.
